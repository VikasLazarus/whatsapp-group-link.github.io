Is Joining WhatsApp Groups Safe?

Safety is our top priority. While we do our best to filter out malicious links, users must remain vigilant. WhatsApp is a public platform, and joining a group means sharing your phone number with other participants.

Safety Tips for Users:

  • Hide Profile Picture: Go to WhatsApp Settings >Privacy >Profile Photo and set it to "My Contacts" or "Nobody" if you are joining public groups.
  • Two-Step Verification: Enable two-step verification to protect your account from being hacked.
  • Ignore Unknown Links: If a group member sends a suspicious link claiming you won a prize, do not click it. It could be a phishing scam.
  • Block &Report: If anyone harasses you, block them immediately and report them to WhatsApp.
